Support and speed up software development

Developing new programs and tools and updating the available software require time, and as we know, time is precious. If you appreciate my work or if you want to support or speed up the the software development process, you can contribute by making donations.

All the software products are designed to have further improvements, and I also have in mind to develop many other free and useful software. But because I have a lots of other activities to do besides programming, it usually takes a lot of time to improve the released programs or to launch new ones. You can change that!

If you use any of my software products and you need further improvements, or you simply appreciate my work and would like to see it keep going, then you can indicate me to focus more on the software development to the detriment of other activities I do.

Use any of the options aside to make a contribution and the result will be a speed up in the software development process. Your support (in any amount) is very much appreciated. Also, please use any possible way of communication to tell me what you would like to see improved, changed or new in the available software or on this website.

Thanks by advance,
Adi Barbu

Donation options

You can make a contribution in the following three ways:

1. Through PayPal

Paypal is a popular online payment system and money transfer. Using it, you will be able to donate any amount of money, with or without a Paypal account (just with you credit card information).

2. Through Bitcoin

Bitcoin (BTC) is an online digital currency that is based on an open-source, peer-to-peer encryption protocol, and is not managed by any government or central authority. From the Bitcoin account (wallet) you can make deposits and then spend or transfer money in Bitcoin currency or in others (EUR, USD, etc.).

About software security and safety

The software from this site is clean of any viruses or any form of malware that you can think of, as long as you download it from this site (and other official pages on external sites, such as Sourceforge, Softpedia and others, which belongs to me). As a way to validate the safety of the available software, whenever I make updates or I publish a new program, I check it first with my local antivirus (currently Bitdefender) and I make a general virus scan online, using VirusTotal.

The thing that I had observed is that some of the antiviruses from VirusTotal.com shows that my software is infected with some sort of malware, but they are not major companies such as Bitdefener, Norton, AVG, Kaspersky, Avira, Panda, etc. but rather unpopular antiviruses. Those are so called false pozitive issues, and they shouldn't be a reason of concern. All of those messages and alerts about my software are simply wrong, being a result of an unreliable mechanism of detecting viruses or malware behavior, in general.
